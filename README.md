## ðŸŽ¯ Problem Statement
The goal of this project is to answer the following business questions:
- How many total accidents and casualties occurred in a given year?
- What is the distribution of Fatal, Serious, and Slight casualties?
- Which vehicle types contribute most to casualties?
- How do road type, road surface, and light conditions impact accidents?
- How do casualties vary across months and locations?
- What is the difference between Urban and Rural accident impact?
